While interning as a UX Engineer at Google, I worked on a design project with UX Design team to create better Android Onboarding Experience.

ANDROID NEW DEVICE ONBOARDING EXPERIENCE

Having a new device is exciting but the setup experience can be daunting. For this project, I worked with UX team to create a fluid experience that is secure, fast and consistent with Google overall user experience.

OBJECTIVES & CHALLENGES

SECURE, FAST, CONSISTENT

With new devices some users forget about their existing accounts and would sign up for new accounts, which leads to the proliferation of a large number of inactive user accounts over the years. One of the main goal for this project is to remind users of existing accounts, while taking into account security issues by only providing necessary user information.

DESIGN PROCESS

RESEARCH AND LEARN

When designing to improve existing solution, it is important to understand the current product and potentials . Partnered with designers, researchers, and software engineers, I tried to understand the reasons of the final implementations. I gathered information on different user groups and their pain points. I learned Google design principles and identified the ones are most relevant to this project and use them as my guidelines in design stage. (trust, personalized, effortless) Technical implementation (engineering implementation - algorithms, logistics and limitations)

Maps & Flow

System Design

Experience Design

USER JOURNEY
