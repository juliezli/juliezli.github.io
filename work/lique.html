Worked as a UX and UI designer on the mobile app design for the iOS and Android for Lique Inc., an ecommerce digital platform that includes social media capabilities. From user journey to wireframes to interactive prototype, I created 100+ mockup design for 8 user flows.

OBJECTIVES & CHALLENGES

OVERVIEW

Lique app has two major components: e-commerce and social engagement. Thus One of the major design challenges is to balance all the aspects of the two parts while keeping the user experience consistent.

CHALLENGES & OPPORTUNITIES

WEARING MUTIPLE HATS

In the startup environment, I performed multiple roles in design realm. As an interaction designer, I made user flows and created 80+ pages of high fidelity wireframes; as a visual designer, I designed user interface mockups and created icon set; as a prototyper, I made interactive prototypes using framer.

RESEARCH & INTERATION DESIGN

Based on the findings and design recommendations of research on users and market, I create user flows for feature interactions. In addition, I conducted stakeholder interviews and performed competitive analysis. I created over 80+ pages mockups for 8 user flows.
